My Story

Hi there! I am a designer from the beautiful south of Germany, super close to the border of Switzerland. Since my Master's degree in Design at the Burg Giebichenstein University of Art and Design Halle, I have been working as a freelance industrial designer on interdisciplinary projects and have since evolved more and more from a physical to a digital product designer.

I recently completed a certified, job-ready training in User Experience Design on the renowned Career Foundry Platform. With around 1000 hours of intensive engagement with the whole design thinking process, I am equipped with all the skills for new digital challenges.
